Pictelio is an unofficial, third-party Pixiv client for Android. Browse your recommendations, keep up with artists you follow, manage your bookmarks, and view illustrations, manga pages, and animated ugoira works.

Key features:
- Home recommendations feed
- Following feed
- Bookmarks
- Work detail viewer with multi-page and ugoira support
- R-18 / R-18G content filtering with an age gate
- Report and block users or works
- Clean, native Android user experience

Disclaimer: Pictelio is not affiliated with or endorsed by Pixiv. Users are responsible for complying with Pixiv's Terms of Service.

Login: Pictelio requires a Pixiv refresh token to authenticate. See the project repository for setup instructions.

Account deletion: You can clear all local app data from your device settings at any time. To delete your Pixiv account, please use Pixiv's official account deletion process.
